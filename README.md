## 📁 About the Project

Poor posture often causes back pain and neck strain. This tool makes it easy to track your alignment in real time. The software identifies your skeleton on screen and notes when you lean or tilt. By providing immediate feedback, it encourages you to sit straight. You can use it with a live camera, a saved video file, or a photos folder.

## 🖥️ How to Use

The main screen shows your camera feed. The system draws a skeleton overlay over your body.

*   **Camera Feed:** The app selects your primary camera by default.
*   **Posture Analysis:** The detection window displays labels such as normal, bowed head, or hunchback.
*   **Video Files:** Click the Open File button to select a video for analysis from your hard drive.
*   **Image Detection:** Use the Image button to upload a still photo for a posture check.
*   **Adjust Sensitivity:** Use the slider in the settings menu if the alerts feel too frequent or too rare.

## 💡 Best Practices

*   **Lighting:** Ensure your room has bright, even lighting. Dim areas make it hard for the software to track your limbs.
*   **Background:** Use a plain background if possible. Too many objects behind you might confuse the tracking feature.
*   **Distance:** Sit at a normal distance from your screen. The software performs best when your full head and shoulders fit in the frame.
*   **Position:** Place your camera at eye level for the most accurate results.

## 🔍 Troubleshooting Common Issues

**The camera does not show an image.**
Check if other apps like Zoom or Teams use your camera. Close those apps and restart the software. Check your privacy settings to ensure the computer allows camera access.

**The tracking feels slow or shaky.**
Close unnecessary browser tabs or background programs. These use memory that the software needs for real-time processing.

**The software alerts me even when I sit straight.**
Adjust the camera angle. If the camera looks down or up at you, the software might misread your posture. Keep the camera parallel to your body.

**The application crashes on startup.**
Ensure your graphics drivers are up to date. Visit the website of your computer manufacturer to find the latest driver updates.

## 🛡️ Privacy and Safety

This software processes all video data locally on your computer. It does not send your camera feed to the cloud or any remote server. Your movements remain on your own machine. We do not track your activity or store your personal data.

## ⚙️ How It Works

The program uses advanced technology called YOLOv8. This system breaks down your body position into key points like joints and neck. It compares these points against standard posture models to detect deviations. The process happens instantly, allowing for a smooth experience without needing a constant internet connection. You receive clear labels that let you know when to straighten your back or lift your head.
